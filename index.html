    Sightseeing Tours

    Visit wonderful monuments and meet charming people.

    • Lisbon Tour

      Let yourself be enchanted by its unique light that holds you in love, more than 5000 years of history.

    • Lisbon Night Life

      ​An unforgettable experience, rooftop bars, gorgeous sunsets, fado, night clubs and more.

    • Sintra

      Hidden beaches, magic castles, enchanting restaurants and breathtaking landscapes.

    • Évora

      City of an outstanding beauty, filled with historic memories and embraced by its fortress walls.

    • Wine Tour

      South of Tagus river heading to Sado river and visiting Cape Espichel, Serra da Arrábida, find some of the best wines in the World.

    • Freemason Tour

      Lisbon holds a masonic history since late eighteenth century. Visit Interiorem Terrae, Rectificando,Invenies Occultum Lapidem.

    • Templars Tour

      Follow the Templar Knights footsteps to where their mysteries have been hidden. Find all about the history and secrets.

    • Religious Tours

      Portugal since the beginning of the 12th Century has been known as Holy Mary’s Land, such is the devotion that exists to Our Lady.

    • Lisbon Wineries

      Striking cellars and vineyards, with stunning scenery and delicious cuisine. Do wine, cheeses, chorizo and ham degustation.

    • Christ the King

      The Sanctuary of Christ the King (Cristo Rei) is a shrine dedicated to the Sacred Heart of Jesus Christ in Almada, overlooking the city of Lisbon.
